Decentralized Governance

Empowering Community Decision-Making

Decentralized governance ensures that decision-making power is distributed among participants, promoting transparency, community engagement, and reducing reliance on a central authority.

Key Features of Decentralized Governance

  • Decentralized Decision-Making
  • Transparency
  • Token-Based Voting
  • Smart Contracts
  • DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations)
  • Governance Tokens
  • Forking Mechanism
  • Community Proposals
