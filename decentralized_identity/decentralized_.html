Decentralized Identity

Empowering Users with Control Over Identity

Decentralized Identity (DID) shifts the control over personal information to individuals, reducing privacy risks and enhancing security. Users decide what to share, with whom, and when.

Key Features of Decentralized Identity

  • User Ownership
  • Decentralized Storage
  • DID Documents
  • Verifiable Credentials
  • Privacy and Selective Disclosure
  • Interoperability
  • Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI)
  • Blockchain Integration
